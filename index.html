Flagship Project

ACB@50

A landmark celebration of 50 years of African cultural brilliance — our inaugural and signature project.

The Vision

ACB@50 is a multi-dimensional creative and cultural programme celebrating five decades of African creative achievement across art, music, film, fashion, literature, and innovation.

The Experience

Spanning exhibitions, live performances, film screenings, panel discussions, and immersive installations — ACB@50 is both a retrospective and a forward-looking manifesto.

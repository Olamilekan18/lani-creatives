The Vision

Why ACB@50 Exists

ACB@50 is a multi-dimensional creative and cultural programme celebrating five decades of African creative achievement across art, music, film, fashion, literature, and innovation.

It is both a retrospective and a forward-looking manifesto — a testament to where we've been, and a declaration of where we're going. Through ACB@50, visitors experience the full depth of LANI Creatives' capabilities.

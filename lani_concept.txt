 
 
Classified as Confidential 
LANI Creatives website design concept, scope and 
KPIs 
 
Objective: 
The website should establish LANI Creatives as a premium African creative, cultural and 
experience platform operating across storytelling, culture, media, events, heritage 
preservation, creative enterprise and innovation. 
ACB@50 becomes the inaugural signature project through which visitors experience the 
full depth of LANI Creatives' work, from concept to execution, content creation, guest 
experience, branding, and legacy documentation. 
The website should generate future commercial opportunities by converting visitors into 
enquiries.
